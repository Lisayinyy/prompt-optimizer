Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 24, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how prompt.ai ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and shares information about you when you use our Chrome extension.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account information: When you sign in with Google, we receive your email address and Google account ID via Google OAuth (powered by Supabase). We do not receive or store your Google password.
  • Prompt data: The prompts you enter into the extension and their optimized versions are stored in your personal history so you can review and reuse them.
  • Usage data: We collect anonymized data about how you use the extension, such as the types of tasks you optimize prompts for (e.g., writing, coding, analysis) and frequency of use. This powers our Insights dashboard.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve the core prompt optimization service
  • To maintain your prompt history and personalized settings
  • To generate your personal Insights report within the extension
  • To authenticate your account and manage your session

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data. We share your data only with the following service providers who help us operate the extension:

  • Supabase — database and authentication (supabase.com)
  • MiniMax — AI model used to optimize prompts (minimaxi.com)
  • Cloudflare — API hosting and infrastructure (cloudflare.com)

These providers are bound by their own privacy policies and are not permitted to use your data for their own purposes.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely in Supabase (hosted on AWS). We use industry-standard security practices including HTTPS encryption for all data in transit. Local preferences and session data may also be stored in your browser's local storage.

5. Data Retention

We retain your prompt history and account data for as long as your account is active. You may delete your data at any time by contacting us.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Opt out of usage analytics

7. Children's Privacy

Our extension is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes by updating the "Last updated" date above.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: privacy@prompt-ai.work
